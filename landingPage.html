Goals & Mission:

We connect community groups and individuals with travel, cultural, and educational experiences to promote personal and professional growth. We work with organizations in the world of travel to broaden their diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI) efforts.

Our Definition of Travel:

When people leave their usual environment, they come into contact with new people, new sights, new experiences, and new ideas.That exposure allows individuals to learn more about themselves and the world around them, creating greater internal and societal awareness.

Services:

With a holistic approach to DEI, we can be a resource around general DEI issues or to help connect you with relevant communities/organizations. Our comprehensive DEI assessments help organizations set you on a path to be Certified by Travel Unity.
